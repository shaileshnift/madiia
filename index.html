General Information

Saraswati Gyan Inter College is co-ed nursery to 12 day school at Fatehabad Agra Uttar Pradesh. Saraswati Gyan Mandir inter College founded in 1982 by Rekha Yadav and family with the motive of providing better education in suburbs of Agra. Saraswati Gyan Mandir Inter College affiliated by Uttar Pradesh Madhyamik Shiksha Board, School providing quality Education, with modern facility and technology
